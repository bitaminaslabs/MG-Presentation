Best Practice: Cardiac Surgery

Anticoagulation Recommendation

Patients undergoing cardiac surgery often take antiplatelet or anticoagulant drugs long-term, which weakens their blood clotting ability.

Heparin is used to prevent clotting. Therefore, cardiac surgery patients do not require additional heparin during MG treatment. Use only saline solution for the priming phase.
